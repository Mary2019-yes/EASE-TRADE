How It Works

We provide a simple and secure escrow service for online transactions.

Step 1: Agreement

Both parties agree to the terms of the transaction.

Step 2: Deposit

The buyer deposits funds with Simplifyd Biz.

Step 3: Fulfillment

The seller delivers the product or service to the buyer.

Step 4: Release

Once the buyer confirms, funds are released to the seller.
